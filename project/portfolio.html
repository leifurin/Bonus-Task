About me

My name is Zhanna. I am 19 years old. I am a photographer. I started with product photography and eventually found myself here. I think it's because I love playing with colors and concepts. By nature, I'm a collaborator. I just can't imagine doing my shoots alone! Right now I am head of "Zhasa Club" at SDU. I think photography is an important part of our lives. Photography can only be important though if we are in the moment and not just shooting the look of something.
